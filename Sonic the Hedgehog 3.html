⭐ 7/10

In theaters Friday, Dec. 20

If one thing is for certain, people love Sonic the Hedgehog. The first two films in the video game adaptation franchise were a box office success, making a combined $725 million worldwide. This winter, director Jeff Fowler will attempt to catch lightning in a bottle once again with Sonic the Hedgehog 3. Little is known about the plot for this third outing, but online rumors suggest the movie may take inspiration from Sonic Adventure 2, the 2001 platformer that sees Sonic, Tails, and Knuckles take on Dr. Robotnik’s new secret weapon: Shadow the Hedgehog. All of the main cast will reprise their roles, including James Marsden, Jim Carey, Ben Schwartz, Colleen O'Shaughnessey, and Idris Elba, while Keanu Reeves will join Sonic 3 to lend his voice to Shadow, in a move that seems to have delighted fans the world over.
