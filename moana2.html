In theaters Wednesday, Nov. 27

Moana reunites with Maui after a mysterious call from her ancestors sets her off on a new high seas adventure. The sequel was inevitable, especially considering Moana was the most streamed movie of 2023—not bad for a movie that was released in 2016.

Auli'i Cravalho heads up the returning voice cast alongside Dwayne Johnson, with Temuera Morrison as Chief Tui, Rachel House as Gramma Tala, and Alan Tudyk as Heihei the rooster. And did you know the live-action movie is filming currently?
